Our Story

Founded in Paris in 2014, Aurélie was born from a single idea: that a fragrance should feel like a memory you've never lived. Our founder, a third-generation perfumer, spent years studying in Grasse before opening the first Aurélie boutique on Rue Saint-Honoré.

Today, we ship our handcrafted perfumes to over 30 countries — but every bottle is still composed by hand in our Paris atelier.
